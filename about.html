About the Game:

4096 is a game that took inspiration from 2048, which is quite popular among Batch 2027. Due to this, the developers decided to add their own spin to the game through elements from HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

2048 is known for its challenge and difficulty, which is expanded upon in 4096, where instead of a 4x4 grid, we have a 5x5, and the goal is to reach the 4096 tile, not 2048.

More about 2048

Rules:

• The game opens with a grid arranged in a 5x5.

• A new tile with a value of 2 (90% chance) or 4 (10% chance) will appear in a random empty tile on the grid following each move.

• Create larger numbers by sliding tiles of the same value. When put together, the value will double. (A 2 and a 2 makes 4)

• The goal is to combine tiles so that 4096 appears on a single tile.

Strategies:

• To make it easier, ensure that your highest tile remains in one of the grid's corners. This tactic allows you to keep valuable tiles out of the grid's center.

• It is also good to limit the amount of directions you push in. By simply choosing to not press the up key (or any other direction as long as you stay consistent), you can get to your goal much quicker.
